Established in 2013
​Fairfield Investment Group was founded in 2013 as an independent group with $50,000 in seed money. The group was established to bring together superb students that share a passion for investing and understanding the dynamics of financial markets. Since inception, Fairfield Investment Group, through collaboration and group-work, has challenged students through hands-on experience and aided in preparing them for their futures endeavors in the industry.
