Grace Hall

Consumers Analyst

Grace Hall is a first-year student majoring in Finance and Accounting at Fairfield University’s Dolan School of Business. She serves as an Analyst in the Consumers Sector for the Fairfield Investment Group (FIG), where she contributes to the management of a student-led investment fund through equity research, investment pitches, and market analysis. Beyond FIG, Grace is highly involved on campus as the Founder and Leader of the Women’s Run Club, a Finance Representative for Women in Business, and a Junior Analyst for the Fairfield Wealth Management Club. She has gained professional experience as a Finance and Operations Intern at GAB-On! and as a Treasury Intern for the Fairfield University Student Association. Grace was also recognized as the annual PB Abate Scholar at The Island School, an honor awarded to one student worldwide for leadership. In her free time, she enjoys running half and full marathons, traveling, and volunteering at a local marine life conservation project.
