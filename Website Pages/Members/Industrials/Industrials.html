Industrials Sector

Sector Overview ▼

The Industrials sector includes companies in manufacturing, aerospace and defense, industrial services, and transportation. This sector is heavily influenced by economic cycles, infrastructure investments, and global trade. The Industrials team identifies high-quality firms positioned to benefit from long-term infrastructure expansion and economic recovery trends.
