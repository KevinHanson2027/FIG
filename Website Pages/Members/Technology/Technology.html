Technology Sector

Sector Overview ▼

The Technology sector includes software companies, hardware manufacturers, semiconductor firms, internet platforms, and IT services providers. This sector is driven by rapid innovation, product cycles, and shifting competitive advantages. Our Technology team evaluates business models, competitive moats, and long-term secular trends to identify high-quality companies in this dynamic, fast-evolving space.
