Our Sector Teams

Consumer

The Consumer sector focuses on companies that provide goods and services directly to consumers, including retail, consumer products, and services. The team analyzes spending patterns, brand strength, and competitive positioning to identify attractive opportunities.

Energy & Utilities

The Energy & Utilities team specializes in energy producers, infrastructure, and utility providers. They evaluate commodity trends, regulatory environments, and capital allocation decisions across the energy value chain.

Financials

The Financials team focuses on banks, insurance companies, asset managers, and other financial services firms, analyzing balance sheet strength, profitability, and performance across economic cycles.

Fixed Income

The Fixed Income team invests in bonds and other debt securities, analyzing interest rate trends, credit quality, and yield curves to provide stability and income within the overall portfolio.

Healthcare

The Healthcare sector team covers pharmaceutical, biotechnology, medical device, and healthcare services companies, focusing on innovation, clinical results, and regulatory developments.

Technology

The Technology team analyzes software, hardware, semiconductor, and IT services companies, evaluating competitive advantages, product cycles, and long-term secular trends in the tech industry.

Industrials & Materials

The Industrials & Materials team focuses on manufacturing, transportation, aerospace and defense, and industrial services companies. They consider economic cycles, capital investment, and infrastructure trends.

Real Estate

The Real Estate team analyzes REITs, real estate operators, construction materials, and building products companies, focusing on property fundamentals, development pipelines, and input costs.

Marketing

The Marketing team supports all sectors and FIG as a whole by managing branding, digital presence, and event promotion. They coordinate social media, creative assets, and outreach campaigns to grow engagement and visibility.
